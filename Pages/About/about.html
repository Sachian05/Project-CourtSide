About Us

CourtSide is a platform that allows basketball players to find and join basketball games in their area. We aim to provide a platform that allows players to connect with other players and play basketball games in a safe and fun environment. Our goal is to make it easier for basketball players to find games and play basketball with other players in their area. We believe that basketball is a great way to stay active and healthy, and we want to make it easier for people to play basketball and have fun.
